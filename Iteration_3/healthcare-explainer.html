Healthcare Explainer

The Australian healthcare system


The public system

The public system

The public health system is owned and governed by state and territory governments and the services provided are funded by local, state and federal governments. It is made up of number of components, including public hospitals, community-based services, and affiliated health organisations. As an Australian, you can access care within the public health system for free or at a lower cost through Medicare.

The private system

The private system

The private system is owned and managed privately through health service providers such as private hospitals, specialist medical and allied health, and pharmacies. The services you can access through the private system are funded by a combination of government and private entities, including: private health insurance premiums (paid by customers), people paying directly for private treatment, and private health organisations.

澳大利亚医疗系统


公共系统

公共系统

公共卫生系统由州和领地政府拥有和管理，其所提供的服务由地方，州和联邦政府资助。它由许多组成部分组成，包括公立医院，基于社区的服务和附属的卫生组织。作为澳大利亚人，您可以通过医疗保险免费或以较低的费用获得公共卫生系统中的护理。

私人系统

私人系统

私人系统通过诸如私人医院，专科医疗和专职医疗以及药房之类的医疗服务提供商来进行私人管理。您可以通过私人系统访问其服务并由政府和私人实体共同资助，其中包括：私人健康保险费（由客户支付），直接为私人治疗付费的人以及私人健康组织。.

Who delivers healthcare?


General practitioners

General practitioners

General practitioners (GPs) should be your starting point when it comes to health and care. When you first meet with them for a consultation, they will assess any health concerns you may present with, and try to treat your immediate problems. However, if needed, they can also organise relevant tests, and can refer you to specialist, hospital or alternate care. For example, if you have type 2 diabetes and need special help, they may refer you to an endocrinologist (a diabetes specialist).

Endocrinologist

Endocrinologist

An endocrinologist is a medical specialist on the subject of diabetes. They know how to treat conditions that are often complex and involve many systems within your body. You need a referral from your doctor to see an endocrinologist.

Dietitian

Dietitian

A dietitian works with patients with diabetes to develop a personalised healthy eating plan to suit your lifestyle, and individual health needs. They can teach you how to read food labels, modify recipes and even how to order at restaurants. Note: Medicare may provide a rebate on dietitians’ fees if you have a chronic condition and are referred by your doctor. If you have private health insurance you may also receive a rebate, depending on the type of cover you have.

Podiatrist

Podiatrist

Diabetes can increase your risk of foot ulcers and amputations. A podiatrist can check your feet and determine if you have a low or high risk of developing more serious problems. Note: Medicare may provide a rebate on podiatrists’ fees if you have a chronic condition and are referred by your doctor. Private health funds cover some podiatry services.

谁提供医疗服务？


全科医生

全科医生

全科医生（GPs）应该是您保健和护理的起点。当您初次与他们会面时，他们会评估您可能遇到的任何健康问题，并尝试解决您的直接问题。但是，如果需要，他们还可以安排相关测试，并可以将您推荐给专科医生，医院或其他护理。例如，如果您患有2型糖尿病并需要特殊帮助，他们可能会将您转介给内分泌科医生（糖尿病专家）。

内分泌学家

内分泌学家

内分泌学家是有关糖尿病的医学专家。他们知道如何治疗通常很复杂并且涉及到您体内许多系统的疾病。您需要医生的推荐才能看内分泌学家。

营养师

营养师

营养师与糖尿病患者一起制定个性化的健康饮食计划，以配合您的生活方式和个人健康需求。他们可以教您如何阅读食品标签，修改食谱，甚至在餐馆点菜。注意：如果您患有慢性病并由医生转诊，医疗保险可能会提供营养师费用的回扣。如果您有私人健康保险，则根据您所购买的保险类型，您也可能会获得回扣。

足病医生

足病医生

糖尿病会增加足部溃疡和截肢的风险。足病医生可以检查您的脚并确认您患上严重问题的风险是低还是高。注意：如果您患有慢性病并由医生转诊，医疗保险可能会提供足科医生的费用回扣。私人卫生基金涵盖一些足病服务。

Who pays for healthcare?


Medicare

Medicare

Medicare is the government’s scheme to give the public access to health care (paid through your taxes), and it covers:

  • Treatment in a public hospital as a public patient
  • 75% of the fee for various doctors’ services in hospital if you’re a private patient
  • All (bulk billing) or some of the cost of going to a GP or specialist
  • Some (but not all) tests and examinations your doctor orders for you
  • Reduced cost on some prescription medications
Private health insurance

Private health insurance

Private health insurance is an optional cover that some Australians take out. You can choose a level of cover to suit your needs. Private health insurance covers things like:

  • If you are admitted to hospital, private hospital cover will help pay for the costs of treatment and your stay.
  • Depending on your policy, general treatment can include dental, optical and physiotherapy treatments, for example.
  • You could get cover for Ambulances rides for emergency/non-emergency transport.
医疗保险

医疗保险

医疗保险是政府为公众提供医疗服务（通过您的税款支付）的计划，其中包括：

  • 在公立医院作为公立患者接受治疗
  • 如果您是私人病人，则需支付医院各种医生服务费用的75％
  • 全部费用（批量计费）或部分费用去往全科医生或专科医生那里
  • 您的医生为您订购的一些（但不是全部）测试和检查
  • 降低某些处方药的成本
私人健康保险

私人健康保险

私人健康保险是一些澳大利亚人可选的保险。您可以选择一种适合您需要的覆盖等级。私人健康保险涵盖以下内容：

  • 如果您被送往医院，私人医院保险将帮助您支付治疗和住院费用。
  • 例如，根据您的政策，常规治疗可能包括牙科，光学和物理疗法。
  • 您可以获得紧急/非紧急运输的救护车的回扣。
