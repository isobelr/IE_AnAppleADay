Risk Factors

高血压

The risk factors for developing type 2 diabetes are:

  • Age
    The risk of type 2 diabetes increases as you get older, especially if you are aged 45 years or older. That's probably because people tend to exercise less, lose muscle mass and gain weight as they age.
  • Family history
    The risk of type 2 diabetes increases if you have a family history of diabetes
  • Race or ethnicity
    Although it's unclear why, certain people — including those from a Chinese background — are at higher risk.
  • Inactivity
    The less active you are, the greater your risk of type 2 diabetes. Physical activity helps you control your weight, uses up glucose as energy and makes your cells more sensitive to insulin.
  • Weight
    Being overweight is a main risk factor for type 2 diabetes. However, you don't have to be overweight to develop type 2 diabetes.

充血性心力衰竭的治疗方法包括：

  • 减少饮食中的钠
  • 减少液体摄入
  • 从血液中去除多余盐和水的设备
  • 心脏移植和其他手术
  • 进行日常体育锻炼

The good news?

The risk factors associated with type 2 diabetes can largely be mitigated through timely and effective lifestyle interventions.
