Nutritional Guidelines

A healthy diet can help to prevent, regulate, and even reverse type 2 diabetes. And with our tips, you can still enjoy your food without feeling hungry or deprived.

营养指南

一个健康的饮食可以帮助您预防，管理，甚至逆转2型糖尿病。加上我们的建议，您可以享受美食的同时不感受到饥饿或食物匮乏。

Protein

Protein

Healthy protein, such as poultry, fish, and beans should make up the last quarter of your plate. Avoid processed meats like bacon, sausages and deli meats.

Whole Grains

Whole Grains

Whole grains, like whole-wheat bread, quinoa, and brown rice should make up about 25% of your plate. Make sure to limit refined grains (like processed white bread or white rice) as these foods have a more extreme effect on blood sugar and insulin than whole-wheat foods.

Fruit & Veg

Fruits and Vegetables

Fruits and vegetables should make up about half of your plate. Aim for a wide range of both - the greater the variety - the better.


Did you know?

Potatoes don’t count because of their negative impact on blood sugar.

Oils

Oils

Healthy plant-based oils (like olive oil and canola oil) can be used for cooking and on salads. But make sure to avoid partially hydrogenated oils, which contain unhealthy trans fats.

Drinks

Drinks

Drink water, tea, or coffee (with little to no sugar). Limit dairy juice, and sugary drinks like soft drinks.
蛋白质

蛋白质

健康的蛋白质，例如家禽，鱼类和豆类应占盘子最后的四分之一。避免使用熏肉，香肠和熟食肉等加工肉。

全谷类

全谷类

全麦食品，例如全麦面包，藜麦和糙米，应占盘子的25％。确保限制精制谷物（如加工的白面包或白米），因为这些食品比全麦食品对血糖和胰岛素的影响更大。

水果和蔬菜

水果和蔬菜

水果和蔬菜应占盘子的一半左右。目标广泛-品种越多越好。


你知道吗？

马铃薯对血糖没有负面影响，因此不算在内。

油类

油类

健康的植物油（例如橄榄油和菜籽油）可用于烹饪和沙拉。但是，请确保避免使用含有不健康反式脂肪的部分氢化油

饮料类

饮料类

喝水，茶或咖啡（几乎没有糖）。限制乳汁和含糖饮料（如汽水）。
