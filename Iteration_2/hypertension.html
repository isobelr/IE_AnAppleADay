Hypertension

高血压

Blood pressure is the pressure of your blood on the walls of your arteries as your heart pumps it around your body.

Hypertension - also known as high blood pressure - is when your blood pressure is permanently higher than normal. It affects around 1 in 3 Australians, and is one of the main risk factors for heart disease, especially heart attacks and strokes.

血压是您的动脉壁上的血液压力当您的心脏将其泵到您的身体周围时。

高血压-也称为高血压-它是指您的血压永久高于正常水平。它影响约三分之一的澳大利亚人，是心脏病（尤其是心脏病和中风）的主要危险因素之一。

The symptoms of hypertension are:

    *Most people with high blood pressure do not have any symptoms, and may feel quite well. This is why it’s important to see your doctor and have your blood pressure checked regularly.*

    However, a few people with very high blood pressure may experience:
  • Headaches
  • Dizziness
  • Chest pain

高血压的症状有：

    *大多数高血压患者没有任何症状，可能会感觉很好。因此，定期去看医生并定期检查血压很重要。*

    一些血压很高的人可能会遇到如下症状：
  • 头痛
  • 头晕
  • 胸痛

The treatment for hypertension includes:

  • Doing regular physical activity
  • Stopping smoking
  • Improving your diet to reduce salt reduce fat and eat plenty of fruit and vegetables
  • Losing weight
  • Limiting your alcohol intake to no more than 2 drinks per day for men, or 1 drink per day for women with high blood pressure

高血压的治疗方法包括：

  • 进行定期体育锻炼
  • 停止吸烟
  • 改善饮食以减少盐分减少脂肪并吃大量水果和蔬菜
  • 减肥
  • 将男性每天的酒精摄入量限制为不超过2杯，高血压的女性每天限制为不超过1杯
