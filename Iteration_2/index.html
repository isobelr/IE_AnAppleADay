An Apple A Day is lowering the instances of potentially preventable hospitalisation

which stem from chronic conditions by addressing the inherent difficulties and language barriers to the delivery of health services to people from culturally and linguistically diverse (CALD) backgrounds by promoting equity and access to information.


Find out more about us.


一天一个苹果网站的创立是为了降低潜在的可预防性疾病

中起源于慢性病的病例并着重向来自于不同文化和语言背景的人群提供医疗服务和信息以此来解决他们的困难和语言障碍。
