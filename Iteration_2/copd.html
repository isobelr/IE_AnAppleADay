Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

慢性阻塞性肺疾病

COPD refers to a group of diseases that cause airflow blockage and breathing-related problems.

COPD makes breathing difficult for around 1 in 7 Australians who have this disease. However, around half of these people living with COPD are not aware they have the condition.

COPD是指导致气流阻塞和呼吸相关问题的一组疾病。

COPD使每7名患有这种疾病的澳大利亚人中约有1人呼吸困难。但是，这些患有COPD的人中约有一半不知道自己有这种情况。

The symptoms of COPD are:

  • Frequent coughing or wheezing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Excess phlegm, mucus, or sputum production
  • Frequent colds
  • Chest tightmenss

COPD的症状有：

  • 经常咳嗽或喘息
  • 呼吸急促
  • 粘液或痰液过多
  • 经常感冒
  • 胸部紧迫

The treatment for COPD includes:

  • Quit smoking: For people who smoke, the most important part of treatment is smoking cessation.
  • Take medication: Symptoms such as coughing or wheezing can be treated with medication.
  • Avoid lung infections: Lung infections can cause serious problems in people with COPD. Certain vaccines, such as flu and pneumococcal vaccines, are especially important for people with COPD.
  • Use supplemental oxygen: Some people may need to use a portable oxygen tank if their blood oxygen levels are low.

COPD的治疗方法包括：

  • 戒烟：对于吸烟者，治疗中最重要的部分是戒烟。
  • 服药：可以用药物治疗咳嗽或喘息等症状。
  • 避免肺部感染：肺部感染可能导致COPD患者出现严重问题。某些疫苗，例如流感疫苗和肺炎球菌疫苗，对于COPD患者尤其重要。
  • 使用补充氧气：如果某些人的血液氧气含量低，则可能需要使用便携式氧气瓶。
