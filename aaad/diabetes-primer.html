Type 2 Diabetes Primer

Here's what you need to know about type 2 diabetes.

2型糖尿病入门

这是您需要了解的2型糖尿病的知识。

Type 2 diabetes, which affects around
1 million Australians,

is a progressive chronic condition in which your body isn’t able to effectively use insulin to bring glucose into your cells. Uncontrolled type 2 diabetes can lead to persistent and recurrent high blood glucose levels, potentially leading to serious long-term complications.

Symptoms

Symptoms

The symptoms of type 2 diabetes often develop slowly and can be hard to recognise. In fact, many people have type 2 diabetes for years and don't know it. However, there are some key symptoms:

  • Excessive thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Increased hunger
  • Fatigue
  • Blurry vision
  • Slow-healing sores
  • Frequent infections
  • Areas of darkened skin

What's the most common symptom of type 2 diabetes?

Surprisingly, nothing! That's right, most people don't experience any particular symptoms. This is why it's so important to keep on top of your risk factors, and to get checked by a doctor if you are at a high risk of developing type 2 diabetes.

Risk Factors

Risk Factors

  • Age
    The risk of type 2 diabetes increases as you get older, especially if you are aged 45 years or older.
  • Family history
    The risk of type 2 diabetes increases if you have a family history of diabetes.
  • Race or ethnicity
    Although it's unclear why, certain people — including those from a Chinese background — are at higher risk.
  • Inactivity
    The less active you are, the greater your risk of type 2 diabetes. Physical activity helps you control your weight, uses up glucose as energy and makes your cells more sensitive to insulin.
  • Weight
    Being overweight is a main risk factor for type 2 diabetes. However, you don't have to be overweight to develop type 2 diabetes.
Myths

Myths

  • Type 2 diabetes only affects old people
    Type 2 diabetes usually develops in those over the age of 45 but is increasingly occurring in younger age groups including children, adolescents and young adults.
  • People with type 2 diabetes can't eat sugar
    Because diabetes effects blood glucose levels, many people think they need to avoid sugars and foods containing sugar. However, if eaten as part of a healthy meal plan, or combined with exercise, sweets and desserts can be eaten by people with diabetes. The key is to eat everything in moderation.
  • You have to be overweight to develop diabetes
    Whilst being overweight is a risk factor for type 2 diabetes, many people who are overweight will not develop type 2 diabetes while some people who are of a healthy weight will develop type 2 diabetes.
症状

症状

2型糖尿病的症状通常发展缓慢，可能难以识别。 实际上，许多人患有2型糖尿病已有多年，并且还不知道。 但是，有一些关键症状：

  • 过度口渴
  • 尿频

  • 疲劳
  • 视野模糊
  • 疮愈合缓慢
  • 经常感染
  • 有皮肤变黑的区域

风险因素

风险因素

  • 年龄
    随着年龄的增长，尤其是如果您年满45岁，则2型糖尿病的风险会增加。
  • 家族病史
    如果您有糖尿病家族史，则患2型糖尿病的风险会增加。
  • 种族或人种
    尽管尚不清楚原因，但某些人（包括中国背景的人）处于较高的风险中。
  • 不活跃
    您越不活跃，则患2型糖尿病的风险越大。 体育锻炼可帮助您控制体重，消耗葡萄糖作为能量并使您的细胞对胰岛素更敏感。
  • 体重
    超重是2型糖尿病的主要危险因素。 但是，您不必超重即可患上2型糖尿病。
真相

真相

  • 2型糖尿病仅影响老年人
    2型糖尿病通常在45岁以上的人群中发展，但在包括儿童，青少年和年轻人的年轻人群中越来越多
  • 2型糖尿病患者不能吃糖
    由于糖尿病会影响血糖水平，因此许多人认为他们需要避免糖和含糖食品。 但是，如果将其作为健康饮食计划的一部分食用，或者与运动相结合，则糖尿病患者可以食用糖果和甜点。 关键是要适度饮食。
  • 您必须超重才能患上糖尿病
    虽然超重是2型糖尿病的危险因素，但许多超重的人不会发展2型糖尿病，而某些健康的人 体重会发展为2型糖尿病。
